About Us

MEET THE FOUNDER

“My aim, as a passionate goat farmer, is to create a Goat Economy to enrich my community and bring about social change, enabling families to become self-sufficient, as well as teaching them everything there is to know about goat husbandry, including nutrition, animal management and genetics to produce a strong, healthy herd.

Goat breeding will enable our people one day to enter into commercial goat farming, learning about the industry in our model farm.

The Goat Economy Project seeks to ensure that farmers directly benefit from their labour by providing them with training, access to markets, and improved breeds to increase productivity and profitability.

Thank you for taking the time to read about the project and for any support or assistance you can offer to help bring this into fruition.”

Doughlous Koche
Goat Farmer
