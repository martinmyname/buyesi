Our Vision

To be the leading organization in empowering smallholder goat farmers to achieve economic prosperity, food security, and improved livelihoods through sustainable and innovative farming practices in Uganda.

Our Mission

Buyesi Youth Initiative (BYI) Limited exists to provide training, resources, and support to smallholder goat farmers, enabling them to adopt commercial farming practices, increase productivity, and access profitable markets, thereby improving their incomes, food security, overall well-being while promoting environmental stewardship and social equity.
