Demonstration Farm

LEARNING CENTER FOR MODERN GOAT FARMING

Our Demonstration Farm serves as a practical learning center where farmers can observe and practice modern goat farming techniques. It's designed to showcase best practices in goat husbandry, breeding, feeding, and health management.

Hands-On Learning

The farm provides a hands-on environment where farmers can gain practical experience and skills that they can apply to their own farms. Our trainers demonstrate proven techniques that increase productivity and animal welfare.

Research & Innovation

We continuously research and implement innovative farming methods that are both sustainable and economically viable, adapting them to local conditions and resources available to rural farmers.

WHAT YOU'LL FIND AT OUR FARM

Sustainable Facilities

Environmentally friendly housing designs, water harvesting systems, and waste management solutions that farmers can replicate at low cost using locally available materials.

Breeding Stock

High-quality breeding goats selected for traits like disease resistance, growth rate, and milk production to help improve local goat genetics through controlled breeding programs.

Feed Production

Demonstration plots for growing nutritious fodder crops and facilities for making balanced feed supplements using locally available ingredients to improve goat nutrition.

VISIT OUR DEMONSTRATION FARM

We welcome visitors to our demonstration farm to learn about modern goat farming techniques. Whether you're an experienced farmer or just starting out, there's something for everyone to learn.
